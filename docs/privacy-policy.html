Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how ZenShift Japan handles information when you use the mobile application. ZenShift Japan is designed to help users track jobs, shifts, working hours, salary estimates, and monthly income.

Information You Enter in the App

The app may allow you to enter job details, shift records, hourly wages, work schedules, notes, and salary-related information. This information is used only to provide the app's core features, such as shift tracking, salary calculation, calendar views, and monthly summaries.

Local Storage

ZenShift Japan stores your app data locally on your device. We do not operate a server to collect, view, sell, or share your shift, job, or salary data.

Payments and Premium Features

If the app offers premium features, purchases may be processed by Google Play. Payment information is handled by Google according to Google Play's policies. ZenShift Japan does not collect or store your payment card details.

Analytics and Advertising

ZenShift Japan does not use third-party advertising services. If analytics or crash reporting tools are added in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated to explain what data is collected and why.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your job, shift, salary, or schedule data with third parties.

Data Security

Because your app data is stored locally on your device, you are responsible for keeping your device secure. We recommend using your device's screen lock, operating system updates, and backup settings.

Children's Privacy

ZenShift Japan is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Choices

You can delete app data by removing records inside the app where available, clearing the app's local storage from your device settings, or uninstalling the app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us using the developer contact email listed on the ZenShift Japan Google Play Store listing.
